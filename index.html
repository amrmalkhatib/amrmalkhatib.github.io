About Me

I am a postdoc at the School of Science and Technology of Örebro University in Sweden.

My research focuses on Trustworthy Machine Learning, particularly in explainability, Conformal Prediction, and Graph Neural Networks. Additionally, I have hands-on experience with Natural Language Processing, Language Modeling, and Transformers.
